## What Krovos Is

Krovos is the financial advisor, the life coach, the trusted mentor, and the educator, brought together in a single system that knows your complete situation, remembers where you have been, and tells you exactly what to do next. It is the guide you were never given.

The organizing principle is the **life phase**, not demographics. The product deepens in understanding over time. The relationship is the moat.

**The core promise:** Answer a few honest questions about your life. We build your financial system. You follow the path.

| Gap | Definition |

| **Clarity Gap** | Too much information, not enough signal. Information without context creates paralysis. |

## The Four Product Surfaces

**1. Life Compass**: The dashboard. Goals first. What matters most right now, personalized by Financial Mode and life phase. Entry point after Life View is generated.

**2. Life Graph**: The planning center. Two layers:
- Layer A: Understanding (auto-generated from documents, Life View, financial snapshot, burden inventory, opportunity map)
- Layer B: Planning tools (interactive, debt ledger, freedom scenarios, projections, goal tracking)

**3. Life Calendar**: Where everything syncs. Pay dates, bill due dates, debt payoff milestones, life events, children's milestones, open enrollment, tax deadlines.

**4. Krovos Guide**: Always-on AI advisor. Knows the complete Life Graph. The questions that come up every day, answered by something that already knows your situation.

## Financial Modes (Behind the Scenes)

Financial Modes overlay any life phase and power the personalization engine invisibly. Users never see their mode label, they just see the right tools and guidance for their situation.

| Mode | Trigger | What It Activates |
|---|---|---|
| Debt-Free | Total non-mortgage debt > $10K | Debt tracker, payoff strategy, interest visibility |
| FI/FIRE | Savings rate > 25% or target retirement age < 55 | FIRE projections, Coast FI, lever analysis |
| Family | Has dependents | Childcare tracking, life insurance, 529 planning |
| Self-Employed | Self-employment income present | Quarterly tax, SEP-IRA, variable income planning |
| Starting Over | Divorce, job loss, or rebuilding indicated | Emergency fund priority, income stabilization |

## The Competitive Moat

**Layer 1, Life Graph Depth:** Accumulated understanding that deepens over years. Cannot be replicated or transferred.

**Layer 2, Trust Relationship:** People who have shared their lives with Krovos do not switch for features.

**Layer 3, Category Ownership:** Define Life Navigation, become it. New entrants fight the category.

**The Flywheel:** More users → richer pattern library → better recognition → stronger trust → deeper sharing → better Life Graph → more relevant guidance → more value → more retention → more users.
