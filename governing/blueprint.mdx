## Part 2: The Problem Krovos Solves

Most of the things that shape our lives are never taught. School covers geometry but not how to file taxes. Trigonometry but not how to evaluate a job offer. The periodic table but not how to choose insurance.

### The Three Gaps

**The Knowledge Gap**: People genuinely don't know, not because they lack intelligence, but because they were never taught. Krovos builds education into every guidance moment.

**The Clarity Gap**: Too much information, not enough signal. The internet created abundance without understanding. Krovos translates information into understanding for this specific person.

**The Activation Gap**: People know what to do and still don't. Not because they are lazy, because they are overwhelmed. Krovos reduces the distance between knowing and doing through specific next steps and friction reduction.

## Part 3: Product Architecture

### The Life Graph

The core data structure, a persistent, structured understanding of a person's complete life situation. Populated through document extraction and guided intake, and deepens over time as life events update it.

Key sections: identity, income (by person, all sources), housing, investments (by account type and owner), debt, tax, insurance, retirement goals, spending, and life phase context.

### Smart Start: 10-Step Onboarding

Document-first onboarding that reads up to 26+ document types via AI extraction. The burden of being understood belongs to Krovos, not the user.

| Step | Section |
|---|---|
| 1 | Identity |
| 2 | Documents |
| 3 | Confirm |
| 4 | Housing Details |
| 5 | Income |
| 6 | Spending |
| 7 | Investments |
| 8 | Debt |
| 9 | Retirement Goals |
| 10 | Review |

### Life Understanding Report

The recognition moment. Six sections:

1. Your Situation (recognition)
2. Financial Snapshot
3. What You Are Carrying (burden inventory)
4. Your Opportunities
5. What To Do First (priority guidance)
6. Moving Forward

Every section grounded in this person's actual data.

### Navigation Engines: By Life Phase

- **Phase 1, Financial Foundation:** emergency fund, debt optimizer, paycheck allocation, retirement trajectory
- **Phase 2, Household:** family planning, parental leave, children's education
- **Phase 3, Optimizer:** open enrollment comparison, tax planning, career navigation
- **Phase 4, Young Adult:** college decisions, early career starter kit
- **Phase 5, Modular Marketplace:** estate planning, home buying, caregiver support

### The Trusted Guide Layer

Persistent AI with full Life Graph context. Demonstrates understanding actively, never makes the user re-explain. Orients to life phase and active decisions before responding. Ends every exchange with a specific, actionable next step.

## Part 4: Life Phases

Krovos organizes around life phases, not demographics. Where a person is in their life determines what they actually need, not how old they are, how much they earn, or what financial products they own.

Life phases are inferred from the Life Graph, never self-reported, never shown as a label.

| Phase | Description |
|---|---|
| Foundation | Building the base, first jobs, student loans, starting retirement contributions |
| Building | Growing complexity, home ownership, dual incomes, early family formation |
| Expansion | Maximum complexity, children, mortgage, competing financial priorities |
| Optimization | Efficiency phase, debt reducing, wealth building accelerating |
| Pre-Retirement | Final approach, Social Security strategy, pension optimization |
| Transition | Major change, divorce, career change, job loss, inheritance |
| Starting Over | Rebuilding after setback, returning to work |

## Part 6: Business Model

### Revenue Streams

1. **Subscription**: monthly or annual, primary revenue
2. **Modular marketplace**: additive capability by life phase
3. **Partner referrals**: transparent, user-interest-first
4. **Employer wellness partnerships** (planned)

### Competitive Moat

- **Life Graph depth**: accumulates over years, not replicable
- **Trust relationship**: people who share their lives don't switch for features
- **Category ownership**: define Life Navigation, become it
- **Flywheel**: trust compounds with scale

### The Flywheel

More users → richer pattern library → more accurate recognition → stronger trust → deeper sharing → better Life Graph → more relevant guidance → more value → more retention → more users.
