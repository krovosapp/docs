## How to Use the Founder Edition

The Krovos Founder Edition is the canonical master document. It does not replace the individual documents in the Krovos library — it synthesizes and integrates them into a single reference that can orient anyone to the full picture of what Krovos is.

**Who should read this:**
- The founder — as a regular reference to ensure decisions align with the foundation
- Future leaders and key hires — to understand the company before making decisions on its behalf
- Investors and advisors — to understand what kind of company this is and what it is trying to build
- Future stewards — to inherit the conviction, not just the products, of the company

When this document and a business decision conflict, one of two things is true: the decision is wrong, or this document needs to be updated. Both are legitimate. Neither should be resolved by ignoring the conflict.

## Part 2 — The Foundation Stones

The Foundation Stones are the irreducible truths beneath Krovos. If products change, they remain. If strategies change, they remain. If the Foundation Stones change, Krovos is no longer Krovos.

**1. People Are Capable**
The challenge is not a lack of intelligence. It is a lack of preparation. Krovos speaks to users as capable people navigating a genuinely difficult task without adequate support.

**2. Understanding Is the Product**
Not software. Not calculators. Not dashboards. Not AI. The product is understanding. The software delivers it.

**3. The Moat Is Relationship**
A system that has understood a person's life for five years is not replaceable by a newcomer with better features. The relationship is the moat.

**4. Peace of Mind Is the Outcome**
Not better decisions. Not financial optimization. Peace of mind. Better decisions are a means to that end.

**5. Life Phases Are the Organizing Principle**
Krovos is organized around life phases, not demographics. A person's needs are determined by where they are in life.

**6. Understand Before Guiding**
This is the stone beneath all other stones. Guidance without understanding is prescription without diagnosis.

## Part 4 — The Product in One Page

### Smart Start — The Life Understanding System

Document upload across 26+ document types plus a focused 10-step intake. The onboarding that feels like the beginning of a conversation, not a form. Ends with the Life Understanding Report.

Design principle: passive understanding first. Documents tell Krovos more than a 50-question form. Every question asked should be one that cannot be inferred.

### The Life Understanding Report

The first product the user receives. A specific, human reflection of what Krovos now understands about their situation. Organized as: Your Situation (recognition) → Financial Snapshot → What You Are Carrying → Your Opportunities → What To Do First → Moving Forward.

**The test:** does the user experience "that's exactly me"? If not, the report has failed.

### The Navigation Scoreboard

The ongoing engagement surface. Not a dashboard — a navigation system. Shows what matters most right now: active priorities, approaching decisions, progress markers, open questions.

### The Trusted Guide Layer

The AI interaction system. Maintains persistent memory of the Life Graph. Demonstrates understanding actively — never makes the user re-explain their situation.

### V1 — The Smallest Complete Krovos

Smart Start + Life Understanding Report + Navigation Scoreboard (planned) + Trusted Guide Layer (planned).

V1 succeeds when a user experiences: recognition ("that's exactly me"), clarity ("now I know what to focus on"), and relief ("I feel less overwhelmed").

## Part 5 — The Business Model

### Revenue Streams
- **Subscription (primary)** — monthly or annual access to the core platform
- **Modular marketplace** — users purchase additional capability modules as life phases require
- **Partner referrals** — transparent, user-aligned connections to trusted partners
- **Employer wellness partnerships** — Krovos as an employee benefit (planned)

### Revenue Philosophy

Krovos only succeeds when its users succeed. The business model must remain aligned with user wellbeing. Krovos cannot profit from confusion. Partner recommendations are driven by user fit, not partner economics.

### The Competitive Moat

- **Life Graph depth** — accumulated understanding that deepens over years
- **Trust relationship** — people who have shared their lives don't switch for features
- **Category ownership** — if Krovos defines Life Navigation, new entrants fight the category

### The Flywheel

More users → richer pattern library → more accurate recognition → stronger trust → deeper sharing → better Life Graph → more relevant guidance → more value → more retention → more users.
