## Anti-Direction Guardrails

Reject any generation that includes:

- Neon accents, glowing digital elements, or futuristic light effects
- Charts, graphs, dashboards, or data visualizations of any kind
- Smiling people, stock-photo poses, or lifestyle photography conventions
- Glowing orbs, particle effects, or abstract light blobs
- White, clinical, or sterile backgrounds
- Stacks of paperwork, paycheck imagery, or accounting-office aesthetics
- Progress bars, checklists, gamification UI, or productivity app visuals

**Positive test before approving any generation:**
The image should feel like the brilliant mentor you never had — warm without being saccharine, honest without being cold, specific without being overwhelming. It should leave you knowing what to do next, even before a single word has been read.

If the image fails this test, regenerate.
